8 Things To Know About Working At L’Oréal

L’Oréal may be a 110-year-old company, but we’re very far from being a cold and corporate workplace. We like to think that being present in 150 countries and working with over 86,000 employees worldwide from 158 nationalities injects a lot of personality and colour to who we are! And despite our global presence, the L’Oréal spirit can be felt everywhere!

You’ll make mistakes that’s okay!
At L’Oréal, you don’t have to be afraid to make mistakes. We strongly believe that experimenting and failing are inevitable to create the best innovations. We try, we fail, we learn and we try again.
Your colleagues will be your family
Teamwork, encouragement of open discussions and team bonding opportunities allow us to create great proximity to our fellow coworkers.
Food matters. A lot.
We really embrace our “lunch culture”, which basically consists of meeting new people over lunch to get to know each other more. It’s a great way to connect with others, exchange ideas, projects and learn about different aspects of the business beyond one’s scope.
We wear sneakers at the office
If you feel more comfortable in sneakers, then feel free to show them off at work! Same goes for leather boots or glittery heels. Basically, dress and express yourself however you wish!
Wellness is the basis of a beauty life
We all know rooftops are all the rage and yoga too. Just like we do for our products, we try to embrace trends in our workplace too. So, we started by organising some yoga classes on our office rooftops.
LinkedIn is not only place to network
Coffees are great opportunities to develop continuous relationships and to get to know different people. In fact, when a newcomer joins L’Oréal, their manager will share a list of people who’ll be important to connect with as part of their integration period.
No such thing as a linear career path
Many of our projects are cross-functional as developing talents also implies to offer them a global vision of our company and its métiers. Skills can always be learnt, as long as the passion to do so is there.
We have our own World Cup
It all started as an initiative from our employees who were passionate about sport and wanted to create an event where they would have the opportunity to meet lots of L’Oréal people from around the world. Our teams actually compete in various games all year long to get trained and ready for the big event.

Why not find your next adventure with us at L’Oréal?
